GCAS Admission

  • What is GCAS

    • GCAS (Gujarat Common Admission Services) is the official online portal for registering and applying for courses at The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda. It is mandatory for all applicants to register on this portal.

  • Registration Process:

    • Visit the official GCAS portal: https://gcas.gujgov.edu.in/
    • Create an account and complete your profile.
    • Select the course(s) you wish to apply for.
    • Submit the required documents and details.
    • Pay the Registration Fee: After registration, you must pay the registration fee for the desired course. The payment can be made via the GCAS portal. Ensure to keep a receipt of the payment for your records.

  • Admission Timeline:

    • Bachelor’s Programs: 15th March 2025 – 2nd April 2025
    • Master’s Programs: 1st May 2025 – 31st May 2025

  • Important Notes:

    • Registering on the GCAS portal is mandatory for all applicants.
    • Even if your course does not require an entrance test, you must still complete the GCAS registration.
    • Paying the registration fee does not guarantee admission; it is part of the application process.
